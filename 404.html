Our Factory

A Shining Beacon of Innovation & Quality

PDV Metals Limited is a group enterprise specializing in non-ferrous metals, covering the full industrial chain from mining, processing, smelting, to finished product production.

Located in the Lusaka Multi-Facility Economic Zone in Zambia, the steel plant was constructed in 2023 and has a production capacity of 300,000 tons.

The plant employs a unique, modern refining process and a comprehensive automated control system to produce high-quality steel, which is extensively used in industrial and civil construction, including highways, bridges, and other high-end areas. The first phase of production is focused on construction rebar, while the second phase will produce angle steel and channel steel.
